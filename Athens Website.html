Athens, Greece

Welcome to Athens

Athens is the capital and largest city of Greece, and one of the oldest cities in the world with a recorded history of over 3,400 years. It is famous for its ancient landmarks and monuments, such as the Acropolis and the Parthenon, as well as its vibrant culture and nightlife.

Whether you're interested in history, art, or just relaxing and enjoying the Mediterranean climate, Athens has something for everyone. Explore the city's many museums and galleries, sample delicious Greek cuisine at one of the many restaurants and tavernas, or take a stroll at night.
